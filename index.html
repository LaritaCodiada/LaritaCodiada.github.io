Hey, it's me, Lara.
Also known as "LaraCode","Laronchis" and many many more.

For the last year i've been working on a little proyect called "The chatrooms"

First developed in Clickteam Fusion 2.5, it was an app where you could hop in to whatever room you'd like and chat with strangers online, taking the concept
from an area on the internet or other computer network where users can communicate, typically one dedicated to a particular topic.

I took that concept and turned it into a little thingy, with 3 channels, the red channel, the green channel, and the blue channel.

it was very basic, and included most of the classical things in the app like the server call sound, the success sound, etc...

and one of them is the most iconic of them all: the "weaow"

The popularity among my friends group grew and got it some fame and admiration.

I continued to make updates, eventually adding unlimited rooms, only being identified with name and version.

The last version made in Fusion 2.5 of "The Chatrooms" was called "The Chatrooms 2", which included things like the "MEGA VIBING ROOM" and name tags.

 it was really looking good until i realized Fusion 2.5 wasnt giving me enough materials to continue with the process.

I stopped the proyect until i could find a new engine to use and remake it.

Eventually, i found Construct 2.

It seemed glorious, i tried EVERYTHING, every plugin, every event, every possibility, and i loved it.

I went ahead and used it for a while so i could get more experience on the engine, and it would just fascinate me more each time.

Eventually, i decided to bring back the old and dusty project i had.

The Chatrooms.

I added commands, profile pictures, backgrounds, effects, and many many more.

I feel proud.
